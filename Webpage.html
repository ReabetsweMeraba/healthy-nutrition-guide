Nutrition and Endurance Nutrition

How to aim for a balanced diet ?

    To aim for a balanced diet that includes a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats, here are a few tips:

  • Include a rainbow of fruits and vegetables.
  • Choose whole grains: Opt for whole grains instead of refined grains.

  • Incorporate lean proteins .
  • Meal planning: Plan your meals in advance to ensure you're getting a variety of nutrients.

How to limit processed foods and sugary drinks ?

    To limit processed foods and sugary drinks, here are a few suggestions:

  • Read food labels .
  • Cook at home .
  • Choose whole foods .
  • Drink water .
  • Limit sugary snacks .
  • Be mindful of portion sizes .

How to stay hydrated?

    To stay hydrated and ensure you're drinking plenty of water throughout the day, you can follow these tips:

  • Flavor your water .
  • Eat hydrating food .
